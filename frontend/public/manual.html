NAV Review Analyst

AI-powered NAV review and exception prioritization for fund administration

Overview

The NAV Review Analyst is an agentic AI application that reviews Net Asset Value calculations for fund administration clients. It detects anomalies dynamically, investigates exceptions, and produces structured review packs for the 4-eye approval process — reducing manual review time by 30-60% while maintaining or improving control quality.

How It Works

Claude Sonnet 4.6 autonomously orchestrates 8 review tools via the Anthropic tool-use API:

1Ingest NAV Package — Parse uploaded XLSX/CSV files containing positions, pricing, and cash flows
2Build Fund Profile — Classify fund type, risk tier, share classes, and AUM
3Extract Market Context — Read market commentary PDF for index, sector, and FX context
4Detect Anomalies — Dynamic anomaly detection using per-fund learned baselines (z-scores)
5Cross-Validate NAV — Compare NAV against prior periods, peer funds, and market benchmarks
6Analyze Position Drivers — Drill into position-level P&L contributors and stale prices
7Score Exceptions — Prioritize all exceptions by materiality, recurrence, and risk
8Generate Review Pack — Produce the final review pack with approval recommendation

Recommendations

RecommendationMeaning
APPROVEAll checks pass, all variances explainable, no material exceptions
APPROVE WITH EXCEPTIONSNAV materially correct but has flagged items requiring documentation or follow-up
ESCALATEMaterial unexplained variances or potential pricing errors requiring senior review
HOLDInsufficient data to form recommendation — NAV should not be released
